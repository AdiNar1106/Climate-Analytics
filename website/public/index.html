ABOUT

Learn about the project!

Climate.DF is a project which will focus on the theme of climate change and more specifically, how climate has changed over the years, and how it will change in the future. We are focusing on different accurate and comprehendable visualizations and portraying how factors like temperature, carbon dioxide, and different natural gases disrupt climate patterns. Through regression modeling and time series analysis, we aim to forecast future patterns of climate and provide methods to control climate change using statistical methods.

We aim to showcase prehistoric climate changes in an interactive Shiny Application where the user can select a start and end data and view different temperature changes over the years. We are interested in showcasing different statistics to portray how climate has changed over different geographies. With respect to time-series analysis, we aim to do a global forecast of temperature to give our viewers an idea of the different global temperature changes and use ARIMA models to forecast future temperature changes. Using effective visualizations of maps, we aim to show how different anomalies like temperatures and precipitation change with different continents or countries.
